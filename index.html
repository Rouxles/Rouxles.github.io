Efficiency in First Block

First Block is very much unlike CFOP. There are many different pairs you can make and in many different ways compared to CFOP. Even though this freedom is one of the very nice benefits about the Roux method, it is one of the reasons why many cubers are not willing to switch to Roux (even though FB is not that difficult to blockbuild)
