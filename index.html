Efficiency in First Block

First Block is very much unlike CFOP. There are many different pairs you can make and in many different ways compared to CFOP. Even though this freedom is one of the very nice benefits about the Roux method, it is one of the reasons why many cubers are not willing to switch to Roux (even though FB is relatively easy to do. It is at around the same difficulty as Cross is for CFOP). There are many great resources that can be found for First Block efficiency, but one video in particular is extremely helpful for people who want to break their CFOP mentality during FB.
