CMLL

CMLL is one of the simpler steps of Roux. You simply just see a case, and then execute the alg associated with the case.

CMLL Spreadsheets

OH CMLLs, 2H CMLLs

These spreadsheets contain what most of the top Roux solvers believe to currently be the best (known) CMLL algs.

If the images do not load, just refresh the page or paste the alg into alg.cubing.net to see what it looks like.

OH CMLL Progression

This is the order of a functional path for learning OH CMLLs and getting used to it. Learn the next one when you feel you're ready to - no need to wait for sub-x.

  • Learn the simpler OH Fingertricks (F*, Fw*, D*, ...) and use them in solves (They should also be used in F2B as well) L moves aren't really necessary for OH CMLLs, so unless you're already used to them from CFOP, there isn't too big of a need to get good at L moves, as the alternatives are nearly always better
  • 2 Look CMLL
  • Full CMLL (without eidos)
  • Full CMLL (with eidos) I have a video here discussing how to incorporate eidos into OH solves, as I feel like forcing yourself to eido everywhere from the beginning isn't the best way to learn eidos, despite that method being the best way for the other fingertricks.
